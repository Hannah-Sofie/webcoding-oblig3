Game

Sustainability bingo

You will need:

The bingo table (click here to go to the printing page, works for A4 paper size).

How the game works:

In this game, you can decide how to play, but your goal is to win bingo by crossing of the boxes on the bingo table. You can choose how to win, by either winning by filling out one line (horizontally or vertically), or cover up your whole table in a full house to win. To cross off one of the boxes, you must first do what the box describes. You can also put a spin on it and say, first one to fill in one line diagonally wins.

Players:

There is no limit to how many people can play bingo together, so go grab some friends or family members, and let the fun begin. First one to fill in one line, or all boxes wins!

And don't forget to show the world when you complete one of the bingo task with #sustainabilitybingo. Have fun and save the world little by little!
